Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main causes of soil erosion?

Soil erosion is primarily caused by wind, water, and human activities like deforestation and improper land use. Natural forces and human-induced factors can both contribute to the erosion process.

How can soil erosion be prevented?

Soil erosion can be reduced by practicing crop rotation, using cover crops, implementing terracing, and following other soil conservation methods.

Why is soil erosion a problem?

Soil erosion leads to loss of fertile land, decreased agricultural productivity, sedimentation in water bodies, and can also increase the risk of flooding.
